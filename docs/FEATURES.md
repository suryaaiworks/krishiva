# Kisan Alert AI - Complete Feature List

## Product Vision

Kisan Alert AI is an AI-powered agricultural intelligence platform designed to help farmers before, during, and after the farming lifecycle.

The platform focuses on prevention, prediction, protection, recovery, and market connectivity.
